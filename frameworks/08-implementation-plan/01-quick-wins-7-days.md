# Quick Wins — What to Implement in the Next 7 Days

> Goal: in one week, generate enough real motion that the framework becomes tangible — not just theoretical.

## Day 1 (Monday) — Clarity

- [ ] **Lifestyle audit** (`../06-mindset-and-wealth/01-freeze-lifestyle.md`) — track current monthly expenses
- [ ] **Cash reserve** (`../06-mindset-and-wealth/02-save-for-rainy-day.md`) — how many months of runway?
- [ ] **Allies inventory** (`../05-people-and-talent/05-allies-vs-energy-drainers.md`) — top-15 honestly evaluated

## Day 2 (Tuesday) — Diagnosis

- [ ] **Traffic-Systems-Skills audit** (`../02-diagnostics/01-traffic-systems-skills.md`) — where is the bottleneck?
- [ ] **1-1-1 audit** (`../02-diagnostics/02-the-1-1-1-framework.md`) — where do you have >1 without a clear performer?
- [ ] **Bottleneck exercise** (`../02-diagnostics/03-bottleneck-finder.md`) — run the "Assume Success" question

## Day 3 (Wednesday) — Customer Discovery starts

- [ ] **3 existing customers** asked for a 20-min call (`../03-customer-discovery/03-talking-to-customers-fear.md`)
- [ ] **Interview script** prepared (`../09-templates-and-worksheets/02-customer-interview-script.md`)
- [ ] First calls scheduled for next week

## Day 4 (Thursday) — Establish memo culture

- [ ] **Memo archive structure** created (`../07-communication-and-marketing/01-memo-culture-wafam.md`)
- [ ] **Memo template** stored (`../09-templates-and-worksheets/01-memo-template.md`)
- [ ] **First memo** written about the most pressing pending decision

## Day 5 (Friday) — Strategic sharpening

- [ ] **Curse-of-capability audit** (`../01-core-principles/01-curse-of-capability.md`) — what do we do because we *can*, not because of leverage?
- [ ] **3 cut candidates** identified (`../01-core-principles/04-growth-by-subtraction.md`)
- [ ] **Current value prop** written in one sentence (`../03-customer-discovery/02-one-value-prop-pitch.md`)

## Weekend — Reflection

- [ ] **Low-phase plan** written (`../06-mindset-and-wealth/05-persistence-aquaman.md`) — before you need it
- [ ] **Skill-stack inventory** (`../06-mindset-and-wealth/03-skill-stacking.md`) — which rare combination do you have?
- [ ] **One memo workflow test** with AI

---

## What's deliberately not in week 1

- Hiring (in 30-60-90)
- Email list setup (in 30-60-90)
- Soft Shop (in 30-60-90)
- Phantom equity (only when the A-player is in the funnel)

→ Week 1 is **clarity + diagnosis + memo setup**. Nothing more.

## End-of-week success criteria

1. You know your real bottleneck.
2. You have a first hypothetical value prop in one sentence.
3. You scheduled at least 3 calls for next week.
4. You wrote one memo.
5. You know your runway.

If 4 of 5 hit — successful week.
