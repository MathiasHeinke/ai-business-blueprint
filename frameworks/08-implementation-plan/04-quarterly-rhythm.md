# Quarterly Rhythm — What Repeats Every Quarter

> A fixed routine that doesn't depend on mood or workload. Done with discipline, you build the business systematically.

---

## Quarterly kick-off (Day 1 of every quarter)

### Half-day block (4 hours)

**1. Diagnostic Checklist** (`03-diagnostic-checklist.md`) — 30 min

**2. Soft Shop update** (`../04-business-architecture/02-soft-shop-strategy.md`)
- Contact 1–2 plausible buyers
- Update the "five things" list
- Adjust quarterly plan

**3. Top-3 definition** (`../01-core-principles/03-attention-management.md`)
- What are the 3 leverage initiatives this quarter?
- Which two cuts make space for them?

**4. Bottleneck question** (`../02-diagnostics/03-bottleneck-finder.md`)
- Assume: top 3 reached. What breaks then?
- Which bottlenecks fix *before* growth?

**5. Allies & 10-10 update** (`../05-people-and-talent/04-the-10-10-forever-rule.md`)
- Who up, who out, who in
- Whom do I invest this quarter's bandwidth in?

**6. Low-phase plan refresh** (`../06-mindset-and-wealth/05-persistence-aquaman.md`)

---

## Weekly routine

### Sunday block (60 min)

1. **Weekly memo** — what was this week, what's coming
2. **Top-3 for the week** defined
3. **Who needs an email this week?** (allies, coach, stakeholders)
4. **Mini diagnostic check:** rough TSS health

---

## Monthly routine

### First working day of the month (90 min)

1. **Diagnostic checklist** filled completely
2. **Customer pulse calls:** 3 existing customers, 20 min each
3. **Financial check:** cash, savings rate, lifestyle drift
4. **Memo archive review:** where right, where wrong?
5. **Update of the 30-60-90 plan**

---

## Annual routine

### Last quarter of every year

1. **Soft Shop run in full** — 5 buyers, full process
2. **Skill-stacking inventory** — what new have we learned?
3. **Lifestyle audit** — where has the nut quietly risen?
4. **Coach reset** — does the current coach still fit the phase?
5. **Allies reset** — top 10 for next year
6. **Strategy memo** — what's the thesis for next year?

---

## What's not in the routine

- Day-to-day operations
- Operational calls
- Standard meetings

→ The routine is *strategic*. Operations stay operational.

---

## Discipline trick

All routine slots are **recurring calendar entries with push reminders**. They don't get rescheduled — they get a memo if they can't happen.

> *No memo, no skip.*
