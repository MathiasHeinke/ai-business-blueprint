# FAQ

---

## Is this just business advice copy-pasted from somewhere?

No. It's a structured **synthesis** of operator principles widely shared by Sam Shvatsa, the Hormozis, and the broader operator community — organized into a usable framework you can drop into an AI assistant. See [ATTRIBUTION.md](ATTRIBUTION.md) for full sourcing.

The original value here is the *organization, the AI-prompt layer, the SOUL.md character, and the implementation cadence*. Without that, the principles sit as quotes on Twitter and don't change anything.

---

## Do I need to be technical to use this?

No. If you can copy-paste, you can use this. The simplest install is:
1. Download the ZIP.
2. Open `frameworks/00-overview.md` in any AI chat.
3. Start asking questions.

See [INSTALL.md](INSTALL.md) for tool-specific guides.

## How long until I see results?

If you actually do the work in `08-implementation-plan/01-quick-wins-7-days.md`, you'll have measurable progress in week 1. Specifically:
- A clearer value proposition
- A diagnosed bottleneck
- 3 customer interviews scheduled
- 1 memo written
- A known runway

The deeper compounding starts at month 2–3, when the memo culture and quarterly cadence settle in.

## Is this only for service businesses?

Most strongly applies to service businesses, agencies, and consulting models — that's the world Sam operated in. But the principles (Curse of Capability, Simplicity, 1-1-1, Memo Culture, Lifestyle Freeze, Soft Shop Strategy) apply across SaaS, ecommerce, and product companies too.

The diagnostic frameworks in `02-diagnostics/` are most service-flavored. Adapt the language but keep the logic.

---

## What if my business is pre-revenue?

Skip `02-diagnostics/01-traffic-systems-skills.md` for now. Start with:
- `03-customer-discovery/` — start with conversations, not products
- `01-core-principles/02-simplicity-as-strategy.md` — find the one thing you'll be best at
- `06-mindset-and-wealth/03-skill-stacking.md` — find your unique combination

The rest applies once you have customers.

## My AI keeps giving generic answers. Why?

Two probable causes:

1. **You haven't installed SOUL.md.** That's the personality layer. Without it, the AI defaults to "helpful chatbot mode." Paste SOUL.md into your system prompt or custom instructions.

2. **The relevant framework files aren't in context.** Don't try to load all 33 at once — load 3–5 relevant to your question. For Claude Projects / Custom GPTs, this is automatic; for one-off chats, paste the framework file content.
