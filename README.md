# AI Business Blueprint

> **A free, open-source operating system for entrepreneurs who want to build a scalable, sellable, sustainable business — and use AI as a co-pilot, not a gimmick.**

Distilled from the operating principles of **Sam Shvatsa** (CEO of acquisition.com, ex-Goldman Sachs, scaled Telos Properties from $300M to $3.4B in 5 years), shaped into a 33-file framework you can drop straight into Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, or any AI assistant — and put to work today.

Curated and published by **[FinLabs LLC](https://finlabs.example)** as a gift to the founder community.

## What you get

A complete decision-and-execution OS, organized into 9 modules:

| Module | What it solves |
|---|---|
| **01 — Core Principles** | Why complexity kills you, why simplicity scales, why subtraction beats addition |
| **02 — Diagnostics** | Where exactly your business is broken (Traffic / Systems / Skills, the 1-1-1 framework) |
| **03 — Customer Discovery** | The two questions that reveal your real value proposition |
| **04 — Business Architecture** | Build to be sellable — even if you never sell |
| **05 — People & Talent** | Recruit superstars without burning out, retain them without giving up equity |
| **06 — Mindset & Wealth** | Lifestyle freeze, skill stacking, the 10-10 forever rule |
| **07 — Communication & Marketing** | Memo culture, decision frameworks, email lists as compounding assets |
| **08 — Implementation Plan** | 7-day quick wins, 30-60-90-day rollout, quarterly cadence |
| **09 — Templates & Worksheets** | Copy-paste ready: memos, customer interviews, JD builder, soft-shop outreach |

**Every file follows the same structure:**
- The principle (what it is)
- How to look at it (the lens)
- How to apply it (the method)
- Concrete actions (checkboxes you can use today)

No fluff. No filler. No motivational poster nonsense.

## Why this exists

Most founders work *in* their business 14 hours a day and never get the chance to work *on* it. AI changes that — but only if you know what to ask it.

**This blueprint is the "what to ask" layer.** Drop the files into your AI assistant of choice and you've got an experienced operator sitting next to you, ready to diagnose, decide, and draft.

You'll learn how to:

- Stop being the bottleneck in your own company
- Find the *one* thing customers actually pay for
- Build systems before you scale traffic (so you don't burn money)
- Hire A-players without writing generic job descriptions
- Make your business sellable — even if you never sell it
- Replace meetings with memos
- Turn a 30-minute call into the most valuable strategy session of the year

## How to install (5 minutes)

The Blueprint is plain Markdown. It works with any AI assistant. Pick your tool:

### Option A — Claude Desktop (recommended for non-developers)
1. Download the repo as ZIP (green **Code** button above) or `git clone`.
2. Move the `frameworks/` folder somewhere stable, e.g. `~/Documents/ai-business-blueprint/`.
3. Open Claude Desktop, in the chat input click the paperclip → upload the README + one or two relevant framework files.
4. Ask: *"Use the AI Business Blueprint as my operating system. Run me through the [Diagnostic Checklist] first."*

→ Detailed setup with project files, prompt templates, and recommended workflows: see **[INSTALL.md](INSTALL.md)**.

### Option B — Cursor / VS Code with AI
1. `git clone https://github.com/<your-username>/ai-business-blueprint.git`
2. Open the folder.
3. In your AI sidebar, reference `@frameworks` and ask the AI to load context.
4. Use the prompts in **[prompts/ai-prompts.md](prompts/ai-prompts.md)** to drive specific workflows.

### Option C — ChatGPT / any web-based assistant
1. Open `frameworks/00-overview.md` in this repo.
2. Copy the entire content.
3. Paste into a new ChatGPT conversation with: *"This is my business operating framework. Use it to answer my next questions."*
4. Then ask your real question — e.g. *"My agency does $400K. Apply the Traffic-Systems-Skills diagnostic to my situation."*

### Option D — OpenAI Codex / Continue / any IDE plugin
Same as Option B. Reference the `frameworks/` folder as project context and use the prompts in `prompts/`.

## Outcome — what you can expect after 30 days

Working through the Blueprint with an AI co-pilot, you should walk out of month 1 with:

- **A one-sentence value proposition** validated against real customer language
- **A diagnosed bottleneck** in your business (Traffic, Systems, or Skills) and a clear plan to fix it
- **A simplified setup** (1 traffic source, 1 conversion mechanism, 1 delivery channel) — instead of the spaghetti most founders run
- **A memo culture** in your team that replaces 50% of meetings
- **A 6-month financial buffer plan** with a frozen lifestyle target
- **At least one A-player hire pipeline** active

After 90 days, you should be running a quarterly cadence (Soft Shop, Bottleneck Audit, 10-10 Forever Review) that compounds — meaning every quarter your business gets clearer, leaner, and more valuable.

## The three sentences that change everything

1. **"What is the one thing — if I took it away — would not be valuable for you anymore?"** → reveals your real value prop.
2. **"Assume I've already accomplished this goal. Does everything else still work?"** → finds bottlenecks before they find you.
3. **"No memo, no meeting."** → forces clarity, eliminates theater.

Three sentences. Internalize them. The rest of this repo helps you act on them.
